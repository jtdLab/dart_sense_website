FAQs

Does VoicePlay work in noisy environments?

No. VoicePlay was developed for quiet to silent environments, especially for practicing. Noisy environments with multiple people speaking simultaneously can lead to instability.

Does VoicePlay work without an internet connection?

Yes.

What happens if an account is shared?

An account is tied to the account holder. Sharing it can lead to the account being suspended.
